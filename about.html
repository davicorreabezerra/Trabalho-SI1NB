About Us

This is Who We are.

Microsoft was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975 in the United States. The first product developed by the company was a version of the BASIC interpreter, for the Altair 8800 computer from MITS. In 1976, Microsoft FORTRAN was launched for computers based on CP / M.

The company entered the operating system business in 1980 with its own version of Unix, called Xenix, but in 1984 a new page was written in history when Microsoft launched Windows.

Among its most well-known software products, the line of Office office applications and the Internet Explorer browser, which has now given way to Edge, also stand out.

Among its main modern hardware products are the Xbox video game consoles, the Surface tablet series and the Microsoft Lumia smartphones. Another highlight of the company in the product sector was in 2013, when Microsoft bought the devices and services division of the Finnish company Nokia.
